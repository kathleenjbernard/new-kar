Let's transform your track

Upload

View instructionsOnce your music is recorded how you like it, bounce all your tracks out separately, zip them in a folder, and share them via Google Drive or Dropbox using kurtis.a.roy@gmail.com. Please also include a few inspiration songs to give me a clearer direction of your goals.

If you're using Logic:
You can send the session files to me in a zip folder. Make sure to delete all unused project files to reduce the size.

If sending from another DAW:
Please make sure all your stems start and stop at the same time. For example, if you have a guitar solo happening at 2:30, bounce the track so there's an empty waveform block starting at the beginning and ending at 2:30. This is a fool-proof way to ensure everything lines up correctly and saves a lot of time.

Mix

View instructionsI will organize and clean up your stems, perform any quantization on the drums and other instruments (if needed) and tune your vocals if requested. Then, the fun begins! I will transform your track from a rough mix to a polished first pass. Once the first pass is complete, it is run through a variety of speakers to ensure it will translate well no matter the output. Once I'm feeling good about the mix, I will send it to you for feedback.

Revise

View instructionsSince music is subjective, as much as we both would like to nail the first pass, this typically never happens - and that's ok! I am not a "what I say goes" engineer - so let's hear your opinions! I offer unlimited mix revisions in order to ensure that we both are satisfied.

With that in mind, please understand that there are certain elements I can't control, such as how the track was produced and recorded. No amount of mixing can fix a poorly recorded song, and I can't turn a drum set into a piano (actually, I can, but you know what I mean).

After discussing and completing mix revisions, I will send you a high quality .WAV file containing your finished mix. Your mix will be at the appropriate levels and contain enough headroom to ensure that a mastering engineer can do their best work.

About Me

Thanks for stopping by! My name is Kurt Roy. I am an audio engineer living in Seattle, Washington. I first became interested in audio engineering after realizing that making high quality music requires more than than simply being a good musician. Now, I have more than ten years' experience recording, mixing, and mastering, and my work has seen over 3 million streams on Spotify and Apple Music.

I would love the opportunity to work on your single, EP, or full album. In my home studio, I use high quality converters, studio monitors, and outboard gear to ensure top-tier professionalism. Your satisfication as an artist is my top concern, so I offer unlimited mix revisions.

For my current availability and anticipated turnaround time, send me an email with the details of your request and I'll get back to you right away. Thanks for your interest - it means the world.
